About

QUEST OF THE MIND

Welcome to Quest of the Mind, a fun and interactive 2D quiz game designed to help kids improve their math skills through word problems and gamification elements!


How It Works

Quest of the Mind presents kids with word problem math questions, and they must answer correctly to progress through different trials. With colorful graphics, friendly animations, and a kid-friendly interface, learning math feels like an adventure rather than a chore!
